My Friends


My friend Lara has been instrumental in my Rubik's Cube journey, patiently guiding me through solving techniques and helping me achieve a personal best of 25 seconds on a 3x3 cube. In addition to Rubik's Cube, Lara introduced me to the world of Yu-Gi-Oh!, where I've developed a passion for playing with a ninja deck. Lara's expertise in both Rubik's Cube solving and Yu-Gi-Oh! inspires me to improve my skills, as I aim to one day surpass his abilities and emerge victorious in our friendly competitions. With Lara's guidance and friendly rivalry, I'm motivated to continue honing my skills in both hobbies, striving for mastery and enjoying the journey of growth and improvement.

My friend Zed is a dedicated gamer, always immersed in the world of Honkai Star Rail, where he tirelessly grinds to conquer challenges and level up. His multitasking skills are impressive, as he often juggles playing Honkai Star Rail and Yu-Gi-Oh! simultaneously, showcasing his passion for gaming. Beyond gaming, Zed enjoys delving into manga, where he finds captivating stories and characters to explore. However, his taste in anime characters can be somewhat questionable, often gravitating towards unconventional or eccentric personalities.

My friend Ian possesses a natural sociability and ease in conversation, qualities that complement my own reserved nature. Despite our differences, Ian's friendly demeanor makes him approachable and enjoyable to be around. His love for gaming surpasses even my own, as evidenced by the vast library of Steam games he eagerly shares with me, boasting thousands of hours invested across numerous titles. Through Ian's enthusiasm for gaming, I've gained insight into a world of immersive experiences and shared moments of enjoyment.

My friend Kevin played a pivotal role during my transition to Baesa Adventist Academy, where his warm introduction to his circle of friends provided me with much-needed companionship in a new environment. Prior to Kevin's arrival, I struggled to forge connections due to my inherent shyness and the unfamiliarity of a new school. However, with Kevin's support and inclusivity, I quickly found a sense of belonging and formed meaningful friendships that enriched my school experience. His kindness and willingness to reach out to others exemplify the true essence of friendship, and I am forever grateful for his pivotal role in helping me navigate through those early days.
