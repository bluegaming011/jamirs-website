Being A Student

Topic I Liked

Exploring HTML for the first time in class was an exhilarating experience, as it unlocked a whole new realm of creativity and possibility in web development. However, my penchant for procrastination often got the better of me, leading to a familiar pattern of leaving webpage assignments until the last minute. As the deadline loomed closer, I found myself pulling exhausting all-nighters, fueled by determination and copious amounts of caffeine, to complete the projects. The relentless cycle of procrastination and sleep deprivation reached its peak when my HTML class coincided with late evening sessions, forcing me to endure nearly two days without sleep to meet the submission deadline. Despite the challenges and exhaustion, the satisfaction of seeing my HTML webpages come to life and the knowledge gained from the experience made it all worthwhile in the end. Reflecting on the experience, I recognized the importance of better time management and planning to avoid the stress of last-minute deadlines in future endeavors.

Chill Spot in the morning



As I wait for my class to begin, I find solace on the bench in front of the engineering building, where I indulge in the calming activity of solving my Rubik's Cube. With my favorite tunes playing softly in the background, I immerse myself in the rhythmic twists and turns of the colorful puzzle, allowing my mind to unwind and focus. The peaceful ambiance, coupled with the gentle breeze rustling through the trees, creates a serene atmosphere that envelops me in tranquility. Amidst the hustle and bustle of campus life, this tranquil spot offers a welcome respite where I can recharge and connect with nature. Each moment spent here is a precious opportunity to savor the simple joys of relaxation and contemplation before the demands of the day begin. Chilling on the bench with my Rubik's Cube has become a cherished prelude to my classes, setting the tone for a day filled with productivity and inner peace..
